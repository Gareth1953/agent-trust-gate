# Paid Pilot Pricing Boundary

P3-M141 introduces cautious commercial entry language for an **Agent Trust Gate(TM) Paid Evaluation Pilot**.

This document explains the pricing boundary. It is not a checkout page, invoice, payment link, payment processor, subscription agreement, procurement commitment, or production service offer.

## Starting-Price Positioning

Indicative pricing starts from **£1,500** for a defined local evaluation pilot.

This starting point is subject to scope, written agreement, availability, buyer inputs, evaluation complexity, and timetable. It is not guaranteed, universal, automatic, or binding.

## Customised Quotation Boundary

Larger, more complex, or customised evaluations may be quoted separately. Any final scope, delivery, price, payment method, timetable, and output must be agreed in writing before work starts.

## No Promise Of Results

The price does not guarantee:

- production readiness;
- successful integration;
- commercial result;
- cost saving;
- buyer acceptance;
- regulator acceptance;
- security approval;
- legal or compliance approval;
- payment or settlement readiness.

## No Certification

The pilot does not provide legal, financial, security, procurement, settlement, identity, authentication, or compliance certification.

## No Automatic Renewal Or Recurring Charge

P3-M141 does not create subscriptions, automatic renewal, recurring billing, automatic access after payment, or automatic paid-pilot acceptance.

## Payment Method Boundary

A payment method may be agreed separately by humans outside this repository. PayPal may be used for manually approved invoices if separately agreed, but this repository includes no PayPal API integration, Stripe integration, checkout, payment buttons, payment links, webhooks, wallet or banking logic, live invoice generation, real payment processing, or settlement execution.

## Safety Boundary

All pricing language is commercial discussion guidance only. It does not weaken the GatePass rule:

**No mandate. No evidence. No verified intent. No signed GatePass. No settlement.**
