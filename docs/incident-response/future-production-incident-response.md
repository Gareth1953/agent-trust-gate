# Future Production Incident Response

Public operation requires capabilities that are not implemented here:

- named primary and backup incident owners
- external alerting and tested escalation
- protected production monitoring integration
- approved log retention and evidence preservation
- tested key rotation, client suspension, rollback, backup, and recovery
- approved customer communication and legal review processes
- incident exercises and post-incident reviews
- a payment incident, reconciliation, and fraud process before billing is enabled

There is no external alerting or customer notification automation. This pack is
local planning only and does not establish production readiness, legal compliance,
security certification, SOC2, ISO27001, GDPR, or payment compliance.
