# Clearing Replay Runner

The replay runner compares two allowlisted local snapshots of a pipeline, evidence bundle, batch result, or receipt verification. It checks decision, caution, approval, allowed/blocked state, receipt, verification, RefusalGraph signals, ledger records, fee placeholders, and explicit safety flags.

Replay is local-only and draft-only. It does not rerun a production workflow or re-execute actions. It writes nothing and performs no live audit, legal proof, compliance certification, public proof, network or blockchain verification, external lookup, tracking, analytics, billing, payment, settlement, deployment, publishing, outreach, webhook, or third-party connection.

Identifiers are deterministic pseudonymous references. Private customer, company, account, wallet, credential, document, endpoint, URL, and message fields are omitted.

Any production replay, live audit, external verification, proof, publication, or commercial use requires technical, security, privacy, legal, audit, publication, and commercial review plus Gareth final approval.
