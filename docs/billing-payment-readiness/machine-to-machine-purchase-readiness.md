# Machine-to-Machine Purchase Readiness

Entitlement upgrade signals exist, but there is no purchase endpoint and no
payment execution. Automatic purchase is disabled.

Future machine purchase requires an explicit authorization model, agent purchase
policy, human approval before enabling, per-purchase and aggregate limits,
revocation, idempotency, fraud and abuse controls, receipts, refunds, disputes,
monitoring, and incident recovery. No account is charged by this pack.
