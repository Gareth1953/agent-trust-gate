# Billing Safety Boundaries

This pack performs no real billing, generates no payable customer invoice,
processes no payment, collects no payment details, and enables no automatic
purchase. Placeholder receipt concepts are not financial receipts.

Before billing, establish customer accounts and ownership, billing terms, payment
provider integration, tax/VAT review, invoice and receipt policy, refunds and
cancellation, ledger and reconciliation, fraud controls, payment monitoring,
support, and payment-failure incident handling.

Nothing here proves PCI, legal, GDPR, SOC2, ISO27001, privacy, payment, or security
compliance.
