# Clearing Evidence Bundle

The bundle assembles allowlisted local references and summaries for a clearing request, decision, receipt, local verification, RefusalGraph matches and query, local ledger records, fee placeholders, report, and optional batch run. It supports future machine-to-machine clearing review without copying private data.

This is local-only draft evidence context. It is not legal evidence, compliance or safety certification, a live audit, public proof, network verification, or blockchain proof. It writes nothing and performs no external lookup, tracking, analytics, billing, payment, settlement, deployment, publishing, outreach, webhook, third-party connection, or action execution.

All identifiers are deterministic pseudonymous references. Customer, company, account, wallet, credential, document, endpoint, URL, and message fields are omitted.

Any live, external, public, commercial, audit, verification, or proof use requires technical, security, privacy, legal, audit, publication, and commercial review plus Gareth final approval.
