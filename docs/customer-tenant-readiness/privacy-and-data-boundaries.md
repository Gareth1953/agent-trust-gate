# Privacy and Data Boundaries

The local readiness model requires no real names, email addresses, passwords,
payment details, or other personal data. Do not put real customer data, raw API
keys, or production identifiers in example tenant files.

Before production accounts, complete qualified privacy and legal review, document
data flows and purposes, minimize collection, approve retention and deletion,
provide customer export and deletion processes, secure access, define breach and
customer notification procedures, and review terms of service.

This pack does not prove GDPR, legal, SOC2, ISO27001, privacy, or security
compliance and does not create real customer accounts.
