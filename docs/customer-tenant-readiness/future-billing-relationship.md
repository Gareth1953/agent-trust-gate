# Future Billing Relationship

A future commercial model may assign usage ownership to a client or tenant and
billing ownership to an account. Plan, billing, payment, entitlement, and account
states must remain separate and auditable.

Billing and payment processing are not enabled. Before billing, define pricing,
terms, taxes, invoices, ledger and reconciliation, consent, refunds, disputes,
fraud controls, payment-provider integration, support, security monitoring, and
payment incident response. Automatic purchase remains disabled until a governed
authorization and payment design exists.
