## What the commercial offer is

Agent Trust Gate™ can be discussed as a local-first commercial review or pilot
around pre-action / pre-settlement trust enforcement for agent-led actions and
payments.

The commercial offer is to review the code, local proof chain, schemas,
receipt model, replay checks, settlement blocker, and integration fit with a
human-approved scope.

It is not a production service, live payment processor, live settlement system,
hosted API, wallet, bank integration, compliance certification, legal opinion,
financial advice, or automatic access product.
