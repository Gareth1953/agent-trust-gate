# Positioning

## Category

Agent Trust Gate is a pre-action trust gateway for AI agents. Its local engine evaluates a described action before execution and returns `ALLOW`, `BLOCK`, or `REQUEST HUMAN` with evidence.

## Audiences

- AI agent developers and platform builders
- Enterprise AI engineering teams
- AI governance and safety teams
- Developers whose tools may act before a person reviews them

## Differentiation

The local product combines deterministic trust decisions, human approval records, evidence bundles, OpenAPI, SDK-style wrappers, an agent manifest, MCP-style examples, and local usage and entitlement signals.

## Boundaries

Positioning must not claim legal or compliance guarantees, real-world identity authentication, production security certification, action execution, payment processing, billing, or automatic purchase. Current availability remains local-only.
