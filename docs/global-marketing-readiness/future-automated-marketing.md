# Future Automated Marketing

Automated marketing is not implemented or enabled. There is no email provider, CRM, social posting integration, ad platform, analytics service, tracking pixel, web scraper, market scanner, or self-learning acquisition system.

Any future automation would require:

- explicit channel ownership and Gareth approval
- consent, privacy, retention, and legal review
- reviewed claims and brand controls
- suppression, opt-out, and frequency controls where applicable
- security review for every integration and credential
- human review for externally published content
- monitored failure and incident handling
- documented data sources without unlawful scraping

Payment and automatic-purchase claims must remain absent until those systems are implemented, secured, reviewed, and explicitly enabled.
