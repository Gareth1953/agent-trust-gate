# Safety and Limitations

- Local-only by default; no public or hosted API is provided.
- No automatic action execution or autonomous authority.
- No real-world identity authentication.
- No legal advice or legality guarantee.
- No compliance guarantee or SOC2, ISO27001, GDPR, PCI, or security certification.
- No payment processing, billing, real charges, or automatic purchase.
- No signup, personal-data collection, analytics, tracking, or email capture.
- Inputs and evidence are not independently verified.

Deploying, publishing, or operating a public commercial service requires separate
security, privacy, legal, operational, support, and commercial review.
