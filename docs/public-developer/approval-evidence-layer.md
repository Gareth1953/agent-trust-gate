# Approval and Evidence Layer

Approval packs prepare a proposed action for human review. Human review records
capture approved, rejected, or needs-more-information outcomes. Verification
receipts record deterministic trust decisions. Evidence bundles combine linked
action, decision, review, integrity, and timeline data for local export.

These artifacts support audit and explanation. They do not independently verify
evidence, identity, authority, truth, legality, compliance, or production safety,
and they never execute the action.
