# Hosted Launch Roadmap

Readiness reports cover commercial, hosted, security, monitoring, incident,
customer/tenant, billing/payment, purchase-policy, and launch gaps. They are
planning snapshots, not production certifications.

Before hosting: complete production authentication and tenant isolation, TLS,
secret storage, distributed rate limits, monitoring and alerting, incident and
recovery exercises, retention and privacy controls, legal terms, support and
onboarding, dependency review, abuse controls, staging and rollback, and explicit
launch approval. A paid launch additionally requires pricing, tax, billing,
provider, payment-security, fraud, refund, and reconciliation controls.
